隐私政策

欢迎使用 Mola相机。本应用由 上海视界象限科技有限公司（以下简称"我们"）开发与运营。我们重视您的隐私与安全。本政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储与共享与您相关的信息。使用本应用即表示您理解并同意本政策；若您不同意，请停止使用本应用。

一、当前账号与社区功能状态

本应用当前不提供手机号登录、账号注册、广场/社区发布、评论、关注或公开个人主页功能。我们不会要求您提供手机号用于登录，也不会在应用内运营向其他用户公开展示您照片或文字的社区功能。

二、我们收集哪些信息

1. 您在使用中产生的内容

  • 照片：您通过相机拍摄或选择用于处理或保存的内容。
  • 文本：您输入的拍摄意图、AI 提示词及其他您主动输入的文字。

2. 设备与日志信息

  • 为保障服务质量、账号安全、支付风控与故障排查，我们及所接入的第三方 SDK 可能收集以下设备相关信息：设备标识信息（如设备 MAC 地址、设备序列号、Android ID、OAID 等设备识别码）、设备型号、操作系统版本、设备设置、崩溃与运行日志等。
  • 为完成微信/支付宝登录、分享与支付功能，相关支付与开放平台 SDK 可能检测您设备中是否已安装对应应用（读取本应用相关的已安装应用信息），用于判断能否唤起微信/支付宝完成支付或分享。
  • 上述信息不包含您的通讯录、短信内容或通话记录。设备标识与已安装应用检测信息主要由第三方 SDK 在其功能范围内收集与使用，详见下文「第三方 SDK 目录」。

3. 传感器信息

  • 为实现取景时的「水平仪」辅助线（提示画面是否水平），我们会读取设备加速度传感器（重力/加速度）数据，实时计算设备的倾斜（滚转）角度。
  • 收集方式：通过系统传感器接口实时读取，仅在您使用相机取景界面时读取，退出即停止。
  • 使用范围：该数据仅在您的设备本地内存中用于绘制水平参考线，不会被存储、不会上传至我们的服务器或任何第三方，也不用于识别您的身份或任何其他用途。

4. 应用内购买

  • 若开通会员等应用内购买，支付将通过应用内支付渠道（如支付宝、微信支付）完成；我们不会接收或存储您的完整银行卡号。为完成支付与风控，支付渠道 SDK 可能收集设备标识、MAC 地址、设备序列号及已安装应用信息，详见下文「第三方 SDK 目录」。

三、人脸数据（Face Data）

本应用不收集、不上传、不存储可用于识别您个人的生物特征数据（如人脸模板、深度图、面部几何网格等）。

为实现仅在设备端完成的取景引导与人像相关功能，我们可能使用系统提供的设备端人脸检测能力，在内存中获得人脸在画面中的大致位置（边界框），用于定位局部处理范围。该过程在您的手机上完成，人脸检测结果不会传输至我们的服务器。

人脸相关数据不会用于身份识别、用户画像、广告定向或与拍摄辅助无关的任何目的。设备端人脸检测产生的中间数据仅驻留在内存中，在处理完成后即被释放，不做持久化存储。

四、第三方 AI 服务（图像传输与用途）

当您主动使用 AI 拍照分析、AI 辅助或风格引导等功能时，我们会将该次功能涉及的图像通过 HTTPS 加密传输至第三方 AI 服务，用于生成文字建议或分析结果。

  • 服务提供方：当前为国内大模型服务商（包括但不限于阶跃星辰、阿里云通义千问 DashScope、火山引擎豆包）。
  • 传输内容：主要为您拍摄或选择用于分析的静态照片以及相关提示文本。我们不会传输您的通讯录、短信内容或与该功能无关的文件。
  • 本应用的目的：获取拍摄引导、构图与光线建议，并在应用内展示给您。
  • 您的控制：不使用 AI 功能时，相关云端分析不会执行；您仍可使用不依赖该服务的本地拍摄与基础功能。

服务方对传输数据的具体处理方式可能随其官方规则更新而变化，请查阅其最新公示文件。

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mola Cam. This app is developed and operated by Shanghai Vision Quadrant Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", or "our"). We take your privacy and security seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information related to you. By using this app, you acknowledge and agree to this policy; if you do not agree, please stop using the app.

1. Current Account & Community Feature Status

This app currently does not provide phone-number login, account registration, plaza/community posting, comments, following, or public personal profiles. We do not ask for your phone number to log in, nor do we operate any community feature that publicly displays your photos or text to other users.

2. Information We Collect

1. Content you generate while using the app

  • Photos: content you capture with the camera or select for processing or saving.
  • Text: shooting intentions you enter, AI prompts, and other text you actively input.

2. Device & logs

  • To ensure service quality, account security, payment risk control, and troubleshooting, we and the third-party SDKs we integrate may collect the following device-related information: device identifiers (such as device MAC address, device serial number, Android ID, OAID and similar identifiers), device model, operating system version, device settings, and crash/runtime logs.
  • To complete WeChat/Alipay login, sharing, and payment, the relevant payment and open-platform SDKs may detect whether the corresponding app is installed on your device (reading installed-app information relevant to this app), in order to determine whether WeChat/Alipay can be invoked to complete payment or sharing.
  • The above does not include your contacts, SMS content, or call records. Device identifiers and installed-app detection are mainly collected and used by third-party SDKs within the scope of their functions; see the "Third-Party SDK Directory" below.

3. Sensor information

  • To provide the "level" guide line while framing a shot (indicating whether the frame is horizontal), we read the device's accelerometer (gravity/acceleration) data to compute the device's tilt (roll) angle in real time.
  • How it is collected: read in real time via the system sensor interface, only while you are using the camera framing screen; it stops as soon as you leave.
  • Scope of use: this data is used solely in your device's local memory to draw the level reference line. It is not stored, not uploaded to our servers or any third party, and not used to identify you or for any other purpose.

4. In-app purchases

  • If membership or other in-app purchases are enabled, payment will be completed through in-app payment channels (such as Alipay or WeChat Pay); we do not receive or store your full bank card number. To complete payment and risk control, payment-channel SDKs may collect device identifiers, MAC address, device serial number, and installed-app information; see the "Third-Party SDK Directory" below.

3. Face Data

This app does not collect, upload, or store biometric data that can identify you personally (such as face templates, depth maps, or facial geometry meshes).

To enable framing guidance and portrait-related features that run entirely on your device, we may use the system's on-device face detection to obtain, in memory, the approximate position of a face within the frame (a bounding box), used to locate the region for local processing. This process is completed on your phone, and face detection results are not transmitted to our servers.

Face-related data is not used for identity recognition, user profiling, ad targeting, or any purpose unrelated to shooting assistance. Intermediate data generated by on-device face detection resides only in memory and is released after processing; it is not persistently stored.

4. Third-Party AI Services (Image Transmission & Use)

When you actively use features such as AI photo analysis, AI assistance, or style guidance, we transmit the images involved in that feature over HTTPS-encrypted connections to third-party AI services to generate text suggestions or analysis results.

  • Service providers: currently domestic large-model providers (including but not limited to StepFun, Alibaba Cloud Tongyi Qianwen DashScope, and Volcano Engine Doubao).
  • Data transmitted: primarily the static photos you capture or select for analysis, and related prompt text. We do not transmit your contacts, SMS content, or files unrelated to the feature.
  • Purpose: to obtain shooting guidance and composition and lighting suggestions, displayed to you within the app.
  • Your control: when you do not use AI features, the related cloud analysis is not performed; you can still use local shooting and basic features that do not rely on this service.

The way service providers handle transmitted data may change as their official rules are updated; please refer to their latest published documents.
